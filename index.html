5 Minute Professional Development Ideas

Algorithm Study Night Practice Problems

Problem 1

  • Sign up for Github.
  • Sign up for Stack Overflow.
  • Sign up for Quora.
  • Create a Medium or Tumblr blog.
  • Join meetup groups in your area. Note: even if you don't have time to attend, meetups are a great way to see what's trending.
  • Create a Twitter account, even if just to re-tweet from relevant engineering leaders.

Find & follow

  • Find key contributors to the languages and frameworks you use. Follow them on online.
  • Sign up for a newsletter that is relevant to your work, or the work you want to do.
  • Follow projects on Github.

Improve your online presence

  • For a quick blog post, drop screen shots of code you wrote, with a small caption.
  • Fix the skills list on your LinkedIn profile.
  • Write a short bio to use across all of your online profiles.
  • Answer a question on Stack Overflow or Quora.

Read

  • Spend 5-minutes on Sunday selecting 5 pieces to read during the week.
  • Read a blog post from one of your favorite tech companies. Check-out this list of engineering blogs.
  • Read 5-minutes worth of twitter feed from an engineering leader you follow.
  • Read a few pages in an engineering book.
  • Flip through and skim a presentation deck from a recent meetup or conference.

Interview Prep

  • Work through one problem in Cracking the Coding Interview or on Code Monk.
  • Read through an interview problem and answer.
  • Brush up on data structures and other core computer science topic areas.
  • Research alternatives to the tech stack you currently work in to be ready to explain why your company uses your current tech stack.
